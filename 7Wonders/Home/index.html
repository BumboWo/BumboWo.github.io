Whyonly 7 wonders were recognised?

The number 7 is considered to be a lucky number in Greece.
That is because this particular number is believed to bring prosperity and symbolizes perfection.

7 Wonders
of ancient world

The first 7 wonders of the world consist of The Pyramids of Giza, Temple of Artemis, Lighthouse of Alexandria, Hanging Gardens of Babylon, Mausoleum at Halicarnassus, The Colossus of Rhodes and the Statue of Zeus.
They are now referred to as the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.

how the wonders
were chosen?

The first list was actually chosen by travelers themselves. They first referred to it as the Sights of the World even if they were all located in the Mediterranean and the Middle East. However, at the beginning of the new millennium, a campaign was raised to choose the new places to be on the list.

the 7 wonders
campaign

The campaign is known as the New7Wonders of the World was led by Bernard Weber. Out of 200 of the most popular monuments all over the world, only the seven will be chosen. For seven years, people all over the world voted through the internet and telephone. Over a hundred million votes were cast since it lasted for years. The winners were announced on the 7th of July 2007.

the new 7 wonders
of the world

The new 7 Wonders of the World are located in different countries from different continents. The Great Wall of China, the Petra in Jordan, the Taj Mahal in India, and the Colesseum in Italy. Along with them are the Christ the Redeemer in Brazil, Chichen Itza in Mexico, and lastly Machu Picchu in Peru. All of them are known to be considered as UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
